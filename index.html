Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how Swish ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your data when you interact with it on Discord.

1. Data We Collect

To provide you with a seamless gaming experience, matchmaking, and economy tracking, we store the following data:

  • User Data: Your Discord User ID. This is required to link your card inventory, rank, and economy balances to your specific account.
  • Server Data: Discord Guild IDs. This is used to manage server-specific settings and facilitate cross-server matchmaking features.
  • Game Data: Your in-game progress, including win/loss records, amounts, card inventories (from standard to Platinum/Diamond tiers), and economy balances.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for the functionality of the Bot. This includes:

  • Running the core card game mechanics and simulation engine.
  • Saving your progress, economy, and inventory across different servers.
  • Pairing you with other players in the park and ranked matchmaking systems.

3. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. All collected data is stored securely and is only accessible by the Bot's development team for maintenance and debugging purposes.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you continue to use the Bot. If you wish to have your data completely wiped from our database (including your card inventory and rank), you can request deletion by contacting the developer via our support server or by using the designated data deletion command (if available).
